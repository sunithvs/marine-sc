Instructions for Abstract Submission


Participants are required to prepare and submit their abstracts following the guidelines below. Adherence to these instructions is mandatory to ensure consistency and ease of evaluation.

Abstract Submission Guidelines

  • 1. Abstract Format:
    A specific abstract format is provided here as a reference. All abstracts must strictly follow this format, including the layout, font styles, and structure.
  • 2. Content Structure:
    Ensure your abstract includes the following sections:
    • Title: Clearly indicate the topic of your research.
    • Author(s): List the full names and affiliations of all authors.
    • Introduction: Briefly outline the research background and objectives.
    • Methodology: Summarize the methods used in your study.
    • Results: Highlight the key findings.
    • Conclusion: Provide a concise conclusion or discussion of the implications.
    Abstracts must be concise and must not exceed the word limit mentioned in the format.
  • 3. Submission File:
    Abstracts must be submitted as a PDF file to maintain formatting consistency.
  • 4. Formatting:
    Use the fonts, margins, and spacing specified in the provided format. Avoid altering the structure or layout.
  • 5. Deadline:
    Ensure submission of the abstract by the specified deadline. Late submissions will not be accepted.
  • 6. File Naming:
    Name your file using the format: [FirstName_LastName_Abstract.pdf] for easy identification.
  • 7. Submission Process:
    Upload your PDF file to the designated portal or email it to the provided submission address.

Note

Abstracts not conforming to the provided format will be rejected. For questions or clarifications, please contact the event organizers.
