Instructions for Poster Presentation


Participants are required to prepare their posters following the guidelines below. Adherence to these instructions is mandatory to ensure uniformity and professionalism in the presentation.

Poster Guidelines

  • 1. Template:
    An example template is provided here for reference. Participants can download the template and edit it directly, maintaining the specified dimensions, font styles, and layout.
  • 2. Creating a New Poster:
    Alternatively, participants may design their own poster from scratch. Ensure the format, layout, and styling closely follow the example template.
  • 3. Poster Size:
    All posters must be designed and printed in A0 size (841mm x 1189mm).
  • 4. Fonts:
    Use the fonts specified in the example template for consistency. Avoid using excessively decorative fonts.
  • 5. Content and Layout:
    Posters should be clear, visually appealing, and easy to read. Include all relevant sections (e.g., title, author(s), affiliation, introduction, methodology, results, conclusion).
  • 6. Printing:
    Posters must be printed and brought to the event in A0 size.
  • 7. Submission:
    If electronic submission is required, ensure your poster is in a high-resolution PDF format.

Note

Failure to comply with the above instructions may result in your poster being deemed ineligible for presentation. For any queries, please contact the event organizers.
